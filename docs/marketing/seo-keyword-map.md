## People Also Ask — scraped themes (qualitative)

Across the 30 pair queries, recurring PAA themes:

- "Is [X] better than [Y]?"
- "Is [X] free?"
- "Does [X] take a commission?"
- "How much does [X] cost?"
- "Can I use [X] for Shopify / affiliate marketing / courses?"
- "Does [X] have analytics?"
- "[X] vs [Y] Reddit" (pulling forum discussions)
- "[X] pros and cons"

### Implication

tadaify's comparison landing pages (tadaify.com/vs/<competitor>) should explicitly answer these PAA themes — use sub-headings that mirror them. This is the single highest-leverage on-page SEO for pair queries.

## Recommended 6-month SEO playbook

**Month 1-2 (foundation)**:

- Build 10 vs-pages on tadaify.com/vs/<competitor> targeting ownable queries (#8, #11, #12, #13, #17, #18, #21, #23, #24, #27, #30 from pair table).
- Include explicit PAA-answer sub-headings.
- Include comparison tables, honest pros/cons, pricing grids.
- Plug every page into each other (internal links).

**Month 2-4 (pitch push)**:

- Pitch Site Builder Report (founder test), Adam Connell (Bloggingwizard), Jotform blog, Zapier blog for next "Linktree alternatives" refresh.
- Submit to G2 + Capterra (seed 15 reviews via early users).
- Launch on Product Hunt with Chris Messina as Hunter.
- Target 20 niche-vertical listicles (real estate, musicians, photographers, coaches, newsletter creators).

**Month 4-6 (niche verticals)**:

- Write 5 vertical landing pages (tadaify.com/for/<niche>) for real estate, musicians, photographers, coaches, newsletter creators.
- Partner content: guest posts on Creator Science (Jay Clouse), Pixieset blog, NotNoise, beehiiv blog.
- If Shopify app ships: launch Shopify-focused content push post-Linkpop.

**Month 6+**:

- Review vs-pages performance; double down on ones ranking 4-10 with more backlinks.
- Refresh every vs-page every 3 months with updated pricing data (competitor pricing changes frequently).
