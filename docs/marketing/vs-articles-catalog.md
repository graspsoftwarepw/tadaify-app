# X vs Y articles catalog — link-in-bio competitor comparisons

> Scope: for each of the ~15 most-searched competitor pairs, catalog the top-ranking "X vs Y" articles on Google. Scraped 2026-04-24 via WebSearch (US, en). Numbers of results reflect first-page organic blend; volume proxies (not Ahrefs precision).
>
> Tiering:
> - **T1** — established SaaS review / creator-econ outlet (G2, Zapier, Buffer, Later, Hootsuite, Shopify blog, Wix blog, ClickUp blog, Jotform blog, TechCrunch, Marketing Brew, Social Media Examiner)
> - **T2** — niche creator-focused blog or independent publisher (Creator Hero, Mobilo Card, Limey, Site Builder Report, Bloggingwizard, Adam Connell, DeanLong.io, Ecomm.design, Clickydrip)
> - **T3** — SEO affiliate farm / scraped-content listicle / product-owned comparison blog (Taplink blog, Linktree blog, Stan blog, Beacons blog, Leap blog, Whop blog, Mobilocard promo, alinkinbio.com, linkinbiotools.com, payable.at, 1big.link, panstag, tempeld, rebrandly, networksolutions — most of these rank but with heavy product bias)
> - **T4 (video)** — YouTube reviews/comparisons
>
> "Affiliate disclosure" column: `yes/no/unclear` — `unclear` means no visible disclaimer but the publisher is known to operate referral links elsewhere on the site.

## Observations & takeaways

- **Linktree wins most brand searches** because of raw users (50M+) + legacy SEO equity. Linktree-led editorial on `linktr.ee/blog` also dominates long-tail queries like "real estate bio examples".
- **Beacons wins most VERDICTS** — its 25% recurring affiliate is the engine behind this. Every small blog writes "Beacons wins for creators" + affiliate link.
- **Taplink SEO-hacks the whole category** — they have a comparison post for literally every other tool, plus their 40% lifetime commission recruits a fleet of long-tail publishers.
- **Stan Store is the fastest-rising challenger** — aggressive content + influencer ops + 20% recurring for subs + creator-set commission on product sales.
- **Carrd wins design/freedom reviews** but rarely wins "best link-in-bio" — it's ruled a "website builder".
- **Gap: no T1 outlet has a strong editorial voice on NEW entrants**. Site Builder Report is the closest thing to a neutral long-form review and the author (Morgan McBride / the SBR founder) tested 20+ tools. This is the #1 pitch target for tadaify.
- **Shopify's Linkpop sunset (July 2025)** left an editorial-opening: Shopify-integrated options are now scattered across bitLink, POWR, LinkShop, Lnk.Bio. tadaify could pursue Shopify app + comparison content.
- **Listicle queries are saturated but beatable** — the top 15 for "Linktree alternatives" is dominated by either competitor-owned content (Stan, Leap, Taplink, Beacons) or old SEO farms. A **fresh, honest listicle from a high-DA outlet** (Zapier, Wix, ClickUp) that adds tadaify would be the single biggest lift we can engineer.
- **YouTube is underserved** — most YouTube link-in-bio reviews are small-audience creators. Mid-tier (20-100k subs) YouTubers who cover creator tools are reachable and most already use Stan/Beacons affiliate links.
