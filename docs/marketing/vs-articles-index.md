## Key findings (for orchestrator / user)

1. **The "Linktree vs X" article SERP is owned by three classes of content**: (a) Linktree first-party ecosystem (blog + support), (b) competitor-owned comparison blogs (Stan, Beacons, The Leap, Taplink, Whop, Creator Hero), (c) SEO affiliate farms. **Almost no neutral editorial voice exists** — Site Builder Report is the exception, and is tadaify's #1 pitch target.

2. **Linktree wins traffic; Beacons wins verdicts**. Linktree's 50M+ user base + brand SEO equity wins the head-term SERP. Beacons wins ~70% of comparison-review verdicts, driven by its 25% recurring affiliate program and broader feature set. Stan Store is the fastest-rising challenger (aggressive content + 20% recurring + influencer army).

3. **Linktree has NO self-affiliate program** that pays cash for Linktree-promotion. The fleet of bloggers writing "Linktree alternatives" is incentivized by Beacons (25% recurring), Taplink (40% lifetime), Stan (20% recurring), Later (30%/1yr). tadaify enters this game at 30% recurring — above median, sustainable, strong pitch.

4. **Shopify's Linkpop sunset (July 2025) left a real gap**. Shopify merchants now choose between scattered apps (bitLink, POWR, LinkShop, Lnk.Bio). None feel Shopify-native. tadaify shipping a native Shopify app is a top-3 product bet.

5. **Listicle-head-queries are saturated but beatable via inclusion** (not own-rank). Target Jotform, Zapier, ClickUp, Shopify blog, G2, Adam Connell / Bloggingwizard, Site Builder Report for INCLUSION in their next refresh. Pitch templates in file 5.

6. **Vertical niches are the fastest wins**. Real estate, photographers, musicians, newsletter creators, coaches — each has a thin SERP and passionate audiences. Ownable in 3-6 months.

7. **YouTube is underserved**. Most link-in-bio YouTube reviews are small-audience creators (< 20k subs). Mid-tier creator-tool reviewers (Think Media, Pat Flynn, Jay Clouse / Creator Science) are reachable. Creator Science in particular — Jay Clouse has invested in Kit, Carry, Kick, Gumroad, Beast; tadaify is portfolio-company-shaped.

8. **Product Hunt launch is free distribution** worth 2k-10k visits in Day 1 + permanent SEO tail. Chris Messina as Hunter, coordinate with existing creators, launch in "link in bio" category.

## Recommended first-90-days execution order

1. **Day 0-14 (pre-launch)**: Build 10 vs-pages + 1 fee-calculator + 1 performance-benchmark page on tadaify.com. Set up G2/Capterra listings + seed 15 reviews.
2. **Day 14-30 (launch)**: Product Hunt launch + Indie Hackers founder diary + r/SideProject launch. Pitch Angle 5 to 3 indie-founder blogs.
3. **Day 30-60 (T2 push)**: 15 pitches — Angle 1 to Site Builder Report + Ecomm.design + Creator Hero + Limey + Clickydrip; Angle 7 to The Leap + Passionfroot + Creator-econ substacks; Angle 8 to Pixieset + NotNoise + liinks + beehiiv + Kajabi.
4. **Day 60-90 (affiliate activation)**: Launch tadaify Amplify (30% recurring). Seed top 10 T2 bloggers (Adam Connell, Dean Long, Creator Hero, Rebekah Carter at Ecomm.design, etc.) with 50% first-year boost.
5. **Day 90+ (T1 push + podcast)**: Based on T2 social proof, pitch Zapier, Jotform, ClickUp, Shopify blog for listicle inclusion. Pitch Jay Clouse / Creator Science podcast. If fundable news occurs, pitch TechCrunch Amanda Silberling + Marketing Brew Phoebe Bain.
