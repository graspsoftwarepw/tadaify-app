# Competitor affiliate / referral programs

> Scope: for each of the 10 competitors, document the affiliate / partner / referral program as publicly disclosed as of 2026-04-24. "Enrollment" column distinguishes creator-facing referral-a-friend style from affiliate-marketer-style programs. The last section synthesizes what tadaify must do.

---

## 1. Linktree

- **Program**: Linktree does NOT run a cash-paying self-affiliate program to promote Linktree the tool. Instead, Linktree **Shops** is a social-commerce layer that lets Linkers earn affiliate commissions from third-party brands (Amazon partnership, commerce stack).
- **Program URL**: https://linktr.ee/products/earn/shops, https://help.linktr.ee/en/articles/8619620-how-to-promote-affiliate-links-with-linktree
- **Commission % / rate**: N/A for self-promotion; Shops pays the brand-set rate (typically 3-15% retail).
- **Cookie window**: N/A (tracked inside Shops).
- **Payout threshold / method**: Via Shops earnings — in-product payout.
- **Restrictions**: US-only for Shops (as of writing).
- **Enrollment process**: Instant for Shops inside the Linktree app; no separate affiliate portal.
- **Creator-facing referral?**: Linktree has historically NOT run a "refer a friend, get $X" referral program for the tool itself.
- **Implication for tadaify**: Linktree is the market leader WITHOUT a self-affiliate program. This means the fleet of bloggers writing "Linktree alternatives" are not incentivized by Linktree; they're incentivized by the alternatives (mostly Beacons 25% recurring, Taplink 40% lifetime, Stan 20% recurring). tadaify can enter this affiliate game at par or better and inherit that audience.

Source: [linktr.ee/blog/affiliate-marketing](https://linktr.ee/blog/affiliate-marketing), [help.linktr.ee/en/articles/8619620](https://help.linktr.ee/en/articles/8619620-how-to-promote-affiliate-links-with-linktree).

## 2. Beacons.ai

- **Program**: Two flavors — (a) Beacons Referral (25% recurring for creator-to-creator referrals) and (b) Beacons Affiliate Marketplace (brand-paid commissions, for creators promoting third-party products).
- **Program URL**: https://help.beacons.ai/en/articles/4705537-beacons-referral-program (referral); https://beacons.ai (for brands)
- **Commission %**: **25% RECURRING MONTHLY** for every referred creator on a Pro plan.
- **Cookie window**: Unspecified publicly; tracked via unique referral link.
- **Payout threshold / method**: Standard creator dashboard payout (Stripe or similar).
- **Restrictions**: Referrer must have active Beacons Pro account.
- **Enrollment process**: Instant via dashboard; no approval gate.
- **Math**: 100 referred creators × $30/mo average × 25% = **$750/mo recurring** for a top creator referrer.
- **Implication**: **This is the strongest programmatic pull in the category.** It's why Beacons wins most editorial verdicts — affiliate-first bloggers and small YouTubers promote Beacons heavily.

Source: [afprogs.com/beacons-affiliate-program](https://afprogs.com/beacons-affiliate-program/), [help.beacons.ai/en/articles/4705537](https://help.beacons.ai/en/articles/4705537), [PRNewswire Beacons affiliate marketplace](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/beacons-revolutionizes-creator-brand-partnerships-with-beacons-for-brands-and-affiliate-marketplace-302423814.html).

## 3. Stan Store

- **Program**: Two flavors — (a) Stan Referral Program (subscription referrals) and (b) Affiliate Share (creators setting their own rates for product affiliates).
- **Program URL**: https://help.stan.store/article/89-what-is-stans-referral-or-affiliate-program; https://help.stan.store/article/378-new-affiliate-share-faq
- **Commission %**: **20% RECURRING** on every referred subscription (Creator plan $29/mo → $5.80/mo; Creator Pro $99/mo → $19.80/mo) for as long as they stay subscribed.
- **Cookie window**: Commission starts after referred user's 14-day trial ends.
- **Payout threshold / method**: Automated payouts via Stan dashboard.
- **Restrictions**: Must have active Stan subscription to earn.
- **Enrollment process**: Auto for Stan users (referral link in dashboard).
- **Affiliate Share (product-side)**: Creators set their own 20-50% commission rates for affiliates who sell their products. This has produced a long tail of Medium/YouTube creator-story content ("How Stan transformed my journey").
- **Implication**: Strong — effectively $5-20/mo recurring per referral is on par with Beacons for high-price-point customers. Combined with the Affiliate Share feature, Stan has TWO levels of affiliate engagement (subscribers + their customers).

Source: [help.stan.store/article/89](https://help.stan.store/article/89-what-is-stans-referral-or-affiliate-program), [schoolmaker.com review](https://www.schoolmaker.com/blog/stan-store-affiliate-program), [ClickBank Stan guide](https://www.clickbank.com/blog/stan-store-for-affiliate-marketing/).

## 4. Carrd

- **Program**: Carrd Referral Program.
- **Program URL**: https://carrd.co/docs/account/referral-program
- **Commission %**: **30% on Pro upgrades and renewals**.
- **Cookie window**: Unspecified (tracked via ref link parameter).
- **Payout threshold / method**: Cashout ≥ $25; IRS forms required for US residents earning > $600/year.
- **Restrictions**: NO paid media (PPC, sponsored posts, paid distribution). NO keyword bidding on Carrd trademarks or competitor brands.
- **Enrollment process**: Free, instant — just generate a ref link.
- **Implication**: At 30% on lifetime-cheap product (Carrd Pro is $19-49/year), the per-referral $ is LOW ($5.70-14.70 one-off, since Carrd is annual and low-friction to resubscribe). Explains why Carrd has less active affiliate content than Beacons/Stan.

Source: [carrd.co/docs/account/referral-program](https://carrd.co/docs/account/referral-program), [elfsight.com comparison](https://elfsight.com/carrd-affiliate-program/), [saasaffiliate.com](https://saasaffiliate.com/partner/carrd-affiliate-program/).

## 5. Taplink

- **Program**: Taplink Affiliate (partner) program.
- **Program URL**: https://taplink.at/en/about/affiliate.html
- **Commission %**: **40% LIFETIME** — partner earns 40% on referred customer's purchases forever.
- **Cookie window**: N/A — customer permanently linked to affiliate.
- **Payout threshold / method**: Publicly disclosed >$350k paid to partners total (publisher claim).
- **Restrictions**: Not extensively published; application-based approval.
- **Enrollment process**: Application form with basic info; manual approval.
- **Implication**: **40% lifetime is the single-highest rate in the category** and recruits a long tail of small-blog content (see Taplink's dominance in SERPs for every "X vs Y" pair). tadaify would need to match OR differentiate on quality-of-pitch if it won't match rate.

Source: [taplink.at/en/about/affiliate.html](https://taplink.at/en/about/affiliate.html), [affpaying.com Taplink](https://www.affpaying.com/taplinkaffiliateprogram).

## What tadaify must do

### Baseline recommendation

Launch **"tadaify Amplify"** at **30% recurring, lifetime of subscription** (matching Carrd's rate but recurring like Beacons, easier math for creators than Taplink's 40% lifetime single-shot).

Rationale:
- 30% is above median of the category (20-25%).
- Recurring beats Carrd's per-sale (our ARPU matters more).
- Matches Later exactly — no one can say we're stingier than a major player.
- Undercuts Taplink on headline % but overdelivers on **total lifetime $ per referral** if our retention is good (which we plan).
- Beats Lnk.Bio (15%) and Campsite (credit-only) trivially.

### Must-have features of the program

1. **Instant enrollment** — no approval gate (Beacons and Stan both do this; application friction is a major complaint in creator forums).
2. **PayPal + Stripe Connect payouts** — PayPal for global creators, Stripe Connect for US/EU pros.
3. **Dashboard with live conversion funnel** — creators want to see clicks → signups → paid in real-time. This is the #1 complaint Stan has addressed well.
4. **Cookie window of 90 days** — generous by category standards.
5. **Branded ref link + custom UTMs** — creators want `tadaify.com/go/creatoraname` not `ref=xxx`.
6. **Marketing kit** — pre-made comparison graphics, pre-written pitch copy, screenshots, badges. (Nobody in the category does this well — differentiator.)
7. **Dedicated affiliate manager role** — even part-time for first 12 months. Stan and Beacons both have one; it's the #1 difference between a program that takes off and one that stalls.

### Content-ecosystem implications

Once the program is live, expect ~30 days of "tadaify vs linktree" / "tadaify vs beacons" content to surface from T3 SEO farms automatically (they sniff new affiliate programs on affpaying.com, saasaffiliate.com, affiliateprogramdb.com). Our jobs:

- **Seed T2 content intentionally** — give Adam Connell (Bloggingwizard), Site Builder Report, Creator Hero, Ecomm.design first-mover access + generous first-year commission boost (e.g. 50% for first 10 referrals).
- **Block low-quality T3 affiliates** from keyword-bidding on "tadaify" brand term (standard Carrd-style restriction).
- **Audit TIER 3 inclusion weekly** for factual errors that could hurt brand.

### Secondary program: tadaify Spotlight (creator-to-creator)

Separate from the SaaS affiliate program, consider a **product-in-product creator referral** (like Stan's Affiliate Share and Beacons' creator-to-creator affiliate):

- Creator A sells a digital product via tadaify → Creator B promotes it via tadaify → Creator B gets commission (set by Creator A, 20-50%).
- tadaify handles payout splits automatically (Stripe Connect).
- Drives organic product creation ON the platform (flywheel).

This is what makes Stan "not just a tool but a creator OS" — it's the network effect in action. Budget 3-6 months of eng for this after v1 ships.
