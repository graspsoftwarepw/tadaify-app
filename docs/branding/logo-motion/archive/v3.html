What changed vs v2

Three deliberate changes from v2 → v3

  • Warm orb 2× larger — diameter is now exactly equal to the large orb's radius (r=20 vs r=40 in the 100-unit space), so the warm orb reads as a co-equal personality, not a small detail dot. At 240px it is 120px wide — impossible to miss.
  • Bounce-back-to-center, not arc-sweep — each greeting point is its own full out-and-return trip. The warm orb goes to N, fully returns to center, pauses, then goes to E, etc. Reads as "addressing four different sections of the audience one at a time" rather than a continuous orbit or spinner.
  • Spring overshoot on the out-trip — the warm orb overshoots the edge slightly (center travels to 26 units instead of 20) before settling back at the true edge position. This gives the "pop to say hello" energy on every outbound leg; returns are smooth ease-in-out (they are not the star of the motion).

Accessibility

The warm orb animation is suppressed via @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce). When the user has reduced motion enabled, the warm orb remains statically centered on the large orb — the fully legible brand mark with no movement. Favicon sizes (16px, 32px) always render static regardless of preference, as the warm orb motion is illegible below ~40px.
