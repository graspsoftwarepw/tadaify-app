Tadaify Logo Motion Mockup

Motion exploration for the orb mark: a big purple circle as the stage / the person, and a small warm spotlight orb that hops around it like greeting the crowd from different sides. The goal is not “loading spinner” energy — it is presence, warmth, reveal, and a bit of stage confidence.

Core Idea

The small orb should feel like a spotlight with personality. It lands, acknowledges a direction, hops onward, and keeps the motion readable enough to feel intentional — like a person on stage waving to different parts of the room.

Hero motion concept

The main orb holds the stage. The warm orb performs the greeting rhythm.

Crowd Sweep

The warm orb travels mostly across the upper arc and lightly bounces at each focal point. Best for a clear, brandable “greeting the room” motion.

Most readable. It feels theatrical without becoming silly. This is probably the strongest version if the motion will be visible often.

Stage Wave

A larger route with more visible pauses and a friendlier bounce. Feels more creator-first and more playful than premium.

Best if the whole brand wants to feel a little more social, approachable, and “hey everyone, I’m here.”

Spotlight Bow

More restrained and premium. The orb glides, pauses, and makes shorter emphasis hops at selected points.

This is the most “premium reveal” interpretation. Less cartoon energy, more design-system confidence.

Full House

The orb performs a longer route around the entire shape, almost like checking in with all sides of a venue before returning.

Strongest `ta-da` energy. Great for launch moments or hero sections, but a bit more theatrical than the tighter versions.
