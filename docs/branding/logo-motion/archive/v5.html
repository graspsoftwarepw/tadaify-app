What changed vs v4

v5 — restraint over expression

  • Single-axis glide, no waving: v4 had the warm orb visit 4 edge points (N, E, S, W) and perform a perpendicular rocking wave at each stop. v5 has exactly one motion: a horizontal translateX sway — left, right, center, pause. Nothing else.
  • Amplitude stays inside the purple orb: v4 traveled the warm orb all the way to the edge of the large orb (±20 SVG units). v5 moves only ±8 units — 20% of the large orb radius — so the warm orb never approaches the purple boundary. The sway is internal and contained.
  • Slow pace with a real rest: v4 cycled through 4 visits × (travel + wave + return) ≈ 11.6 s of continuous motion. v5 has 6.75 s of gentle movement followed by 2.25 s of complete stillness — the logo reads as calm and static unless you watch it for a few seconds. Total cycle is 9 s but feels much shorter because most of that time is quiet.

Accessibility

The animation respects prefers-reduced-motion: reduce. When that media query is active the warm orb remains stationary at center — the logo is identical to the static brand mark. The wordmark tada!ify never animates in any variant.
