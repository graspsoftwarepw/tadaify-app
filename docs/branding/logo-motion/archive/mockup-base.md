# Tadaify Logo Motion Mockup

## Purpose

Explore a motion idea for the temporary `Tadaify` orb mark and its paired wordmark:

- a **large purple circle** acts as the stage / the person
- a **small warm yellow-orange circle** acts as the spotlight / greeting gesture
- the companion wordmark uses **`ta` in primary purple** and **`da!` in warm orange**
- the small circle **bounces, moves, bounces again**, and continues around the main shape
- the motion should feel like *greeting the crowd from different sides of a stage*

## Visual logic

- **Large orb:** stable identity, presence, stage center
- **Small orb:** warmth, attention, reveal energy
- **Bounce cadence:** not random particles, but intentional greeting points
- **Orbit path:** communicates movement around an audience, not loading or spinning

## Variants included

1. **Crowd Sweep**
   - the small orb hops around the upper arc
   - feels like acknowledging the crowd from left to right

2. **Stage Wave**
   - slightly larger movement and softer pauses
   - more playful, more creator-friendly

3. **Spotlight Bow**
   - more restrained, premium version
   - the orb glides, pauses, and bows at focal points

4. **Full House**
   - the orb makes a longer loop around the main orb
   - strongest `ta-da` energy, most theatrical

## Recommendation

The strongest direction is probably **Crowd Sweep** or **Spotlight Bow**:

- `Crowd Sweep` is clearer and more memorable
- `Spotlight Bow` is more premium and less cartoonish

For static logo use, the strongest candidates are probably:

- `Bottom` — strongest stage / audience connection
- `Top-left corner` — strongest reveal / spotlight vibe
- `Right` — cleanest directional balance

The motion should stay:

- short
- elegant
- loopable
- subtle enough not to feel like a loading spinner
