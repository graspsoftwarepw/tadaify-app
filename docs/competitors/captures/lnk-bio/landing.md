# Lnk.Bio — landing page audit

## Scrape constraints

lnk.bio/ returned 403 to WebFetch. Content synthesised from
lnk.bio/all-features (accessible via SERP), third-party reviewers
(Capterra, G2, SaaSworthy, SoftwareWorld, Orichi), and Taplink's own
competitor write-up.

## Positioning

Lnk.Bio positions itself as **"the most affordable link-in-bio
tool"** — price is the headline value prop [source:
https://orichi.info/lnk-bio-review/]. The product is Italian-built
(Milan-based team) and has been quietly operating since 2016.

## Feature surface

- **Unlimited links on all plans** including free — "Lnk.bio offers
  truly unlimited links on all plans, including the free tier, which
  alone sets it apart from competitors that impose link limits at the
  free level" [source: orichi.info]
- **368+ themes and layout options**
- Custom backgrounds + video wallpapers
- **44,000+ Google Fonts on paid plans**
- Custom domain (paid)
- Analytics (deeper on paid)
- Mailchimp integration
- Social network integrations
- Scheduler for time-sensitive links
- Embeddable widgets

Source: [lnk.bio/all-features](https://lnk.bio/all-features) (via SERP)

## Social proof

- Capterra / G2 / SoftwareWorld / SaaSworthy reviewer coverage is
  broad but footprint smaller than Linktree's
- Claimed affordability is the marketing wedge

## CTAs

- Primary: **Sign up for free** → signup
- Secondary: **See pricing**
- Tertiary: **View features**

## Trust signals

- 10+ years in market (since 2016)
- Italian company; GDPR compliant by default

## Growth hooks

- **Unlimited links FREE** — anchor vs competitors with link caps
- **$0.99/mo Mini Monthly** — cheapest paid tier in entire category
- **$9.99 Mini One-Time** — pay once, keep forever
- **$24.99 Unique One-Time** — lifetime all-features tier (headline
  differentiator)
- Lack of subscription coercion is a direct anti-SaaS message

## Segment landings

Lnk.Bio positions generically; not heavy on niche segment pages.
