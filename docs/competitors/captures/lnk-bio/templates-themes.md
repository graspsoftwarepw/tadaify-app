# Lnk.Bio — templates & themes

## Gallery size

- **368+ themes and layout options** — one of the largest theme
  libraries in the category [source:
  https://orichi.info/lnk-bio-review/]
- **44,000+ Google Fonts** on paid plans — unmatched typography depth
- Video wallpapers — a distinctive visual capability
- Custom backgrounds (static image)

## Customisation depth

- Theme selection from 368+ library
- Font picker with Google Fonts full catalogue on paid
- Colour customisation

- Custom background upload
