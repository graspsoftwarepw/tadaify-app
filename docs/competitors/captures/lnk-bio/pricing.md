## The "Unique" lifetime deal

**$24.99 one-time for lifetime access to all features** is the single
most creator-friendly pricing structure in this entire research set.
Direct quote: "The one-time $24.99 Unique plan is the platform's
strongest selling point. In a market dominated by subscription-first
competitors, paying once and owning your upgrades permanently is a
refreshing and financially smart choice for most users"
[source: orichi.info/lnk-bio-review/].

## Free plan

- **Truly unlimited links** (most competitors cap free tier links)
- Basic themes
- Simple analytics
- Lnk.Bio branding
- No custom domain
- No advanced integrations

## Mini ($0.99/mo or $9.99 one-time)

- Removes Lnk.Bio branding
- Otherwise same feature set as free
- Entry-level paid tier for creators who only want brand-free URL

## Unique ($24.99 one-time — lifetime)

- All 368+ themes unlocked
- 44,000+ Google Fonts
- Custom domain connection
- Advanced analytics
- All integrations
- Video wallpapers / custom backgrounds
- Scheduler
- Paid once, kept forever
