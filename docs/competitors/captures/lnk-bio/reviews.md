# Lnk.Bio — reviews synthesis

## Rating snapshot

- **Capterra**: active reviews [source:
  https://www.capterra.com/p/211802/Lnk-Bio/]
- **G2**: active reviews
- **SoftwareWorld**: 2026 reviews page active
- **Trustpilot**: [unclear — Lnk.Bio doesn't prominently embed
  Trustpilot; surface exists]

## Top 5 praised features

1. **Unlimited links on free tier** — "Lnk.bio offers truly unlimited
   links on all plans, including the free tier, which alone sets it
   apart from competitors that impose link limits at the free level"
   [source: orichi.info/lnk-bio-review/]

2. **Lifetime pricing** — "$24.99 Unique plan is the platform's
   strongest selling point" [source: orichi.info]

3. **368+ themes** — extensive design library at the cheap pricing

4. **44,000+ Google Fonts** — typography depth beyond any competitor

5. **Video wallpapers** — unusual design-level richness

## Top 5 complaints

1. **Bland brand / low recognition** — Lnk.Bio doesn't have
   Linktree's mindshare; using Lnk.Bio URL means your audience may
   not recognise the pattern

2. **Basic analytics free tier** — "The free tier analytics are
   borderline useless for anyone making data-driven decisions.
   Knowing you had '1,247 total clicks' without context — no dates,
   no sources, no geography — tells you almost nothing"
   [source: https://taplink.at/en/blog/lnk-bio-vs-linktree.html]

3. **Sparse integrations** — no Shopify, no Stripe-native, no
   Instagram DM automation

4. **Limited commerce / monetisation** compared to Beacons / Stan

5. **Italian-company support latency** [unclear — anecdotal]

## Churn / cancellation reasons

- Creators outgrow Lnk.Bio when they need native commerce or deep
  analytics
- Migration to Beacons / Stan / Linktree for feature depth
- But the Unique lifetime deal reduces churn trivially — creators
  who paid once feel no monthly pressure to leave

## Pricing complaints

- Essentially none — Lnk.Bio's pricing is the thing everyone loves

## Feature requests

- Native payment surfaces
- Deeper analytics (especially on free tier)
- Instagram DM automation
- Shopify integration
- Public API for agency management
