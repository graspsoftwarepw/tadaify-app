# Beacons.ai — pricing matrix

## Scrape method

Direct fetch of `https://beacons.ai/i/plans` returned structured content
on 2026-04-24. Matrix below is authoritative as of that fetch. Some
reviewer sources reference older plan names ("Creator Pro", "Store Pro",
"Business Pro") but Beacons' 2026 taxonomy is **Free / Creator / Creator
Plus / Creator Max**. Third-party articles that use the old names are
stale.

## Tier matrix (April 2026, from direct fetch)

| Tier          | Monthly | Annual (effective monthly) | Annual total | Tagline |
|---------------|---------|----------------------------|--------------|---------|
| Free          | $0      | $0                         | $0           | Build and customize |
| Creator       | $10     | $8.33                      | $100         | Claim your brand |
| Creator Plus  | $30     | $25                        | $300         | Drive sales and partnerships |
| Creator Max   | $90     | $75                        | $900         | Grow without limits |

Annual billing advertised as **"2 months free"** across all paid tiers
(i.e. ~16.7% off). Source: direct fetch of
[beacons.ai/i/plans](https://beacons.ai/i/plans).

## Feature-by-feature matrix (verbatim from /i/plans fetch)

### AI credits (daily allowance)
- Free: **30 / day**
- Creator: **300 / day**
- Creator Plus: **Unlimited / day**
- Creator Max: **Unlimited / day**

### Link in Bio & Website Builder — commonalities
All four tiers include:
- Unlimited links in customizable bio
- Unlimited customization (colors, fonts, styles, layouts)
- Advanced traffic analytics
- Embed affiliate links with 12k brand partners

### Link in Bio & Website Builder — tier-specific
- Creator: **free custom domain** (terms TBD — likely 1st year, per
  khaby.ai reviewer)
- Creator Plus: custom domain for BOTH link-in-bio and store URLs
- Creator Max: same + 10 websites × 10 pages each (multi-site cap)

Website builder page limits:
- Free / Creator: 1 website, 1 page
- Creator Plus: 1 website, 3 pages
- Creator Max: 10 websites, 10 pages each

### Store & Digital Products
- Free + Creator: **9% transaction fee**, unlimited digital products
- Creator Plus: **0% transaction fee**, unlimited products, **Buy Now
  Pay Later via Klarna / Affirm**
- Creator Max: all of Creator Plus + unlimited memberships + courses
  with **video hosting**

### Email Marketing
- Free: **50 sends/month**
- Creator: **500 sends/month**
- Creator Plus: **Unlimited**
- Creator Max: **Unlimited**

### Brand Deals & Media Kit
All tiers:
- Auto-updating media kit
- Unlimited customization
- Media kit analytics
- Create affiliate links

Paid-tier additions:
- Creator: invoicing, rate cards
- Creator Plus: past projects showcase, video pitch upload, contact
  form, gated access, multiple sharing options
- Creator Max: white-glove onboarding, prioritized support (<6 hours
  response SLA)

### Instagram DM Automation ("Smart Reply")
- Free: Smart Reply IG auto-DM (entry-level)
- Creator / Plus / Max: **unlimited** IG DM automations from Reels /
  Posts / Stories

### Affiliate commissions retained
- Free / Creator: keep **91%** of affiliate earnings (9% to Beacons)
- Creator Plus / Max: keep **100%**

## Transaction & seller fee ladder

| Plan | Digital product fee | Affiliate commission retained |
|------|---------------------|-------------------------------|
| Free | 9% + processor | 91% |
| Creator | 9% + processor | 91% |
| Creator Plus | 0% + processor | 100% |
| Creator Max | 0% + processor | 100% |

Source: direct fetch of /i/plans.

## Anchor / psychological pattern

- **"Creator Plus: Drive sales and partnerships"** appears featured on
  the plans page (most-prominent / "recommended" position)
- **Creator Max at $90/mo** is a significant price ceiling — roughly
  3× Creator Plus; the "white glove + NFC card + prioritized support"
  tier justifies enterprise-adjacent price
- **Entry tier at $10/mo** (Creator) is materially cheaper than
  Linktree's equivalent Starter ($8) but Beacons bundles more (custom
  domain); effectively Beacons' $10 competes with Linktree's $15 Pro

## "Hidden cost" observations (reviewer-flagged)

- autoposting.ai cites: "If you sell $1,000 monthly on the free plan,
  you're paying $90 in transaction fees — higher than most premium
  plans on competing platforms"
  [source: https://autoposting.ai/beacons-ai-review/]
- The fee ladder can make Creator Plus necessary faster than the
  sticker price suggests — the ROI math is $30/mo × 12 = $360/yr,
  break-even at $4000/yr of digital sales (vs Linktree's
  $222/mo × 12 = $2664/yr break-even on $2960/yr of sales)

## Discount / student / NGO pricing

- Annual plan = "2 months free" is the only public discount
  [source: direct fetch /i/plans]
- No student or NGO pricing surfaced publicly [unclear — confirm]
