# Carrd — pricing matrix

## Pricing structure (April 2026)

Carrd's pricing is **the most distinctive in the category**:
- **Annual-only billing** (no monthly)
- Four tiers including free
- Price range $0–$49/year (not /month)

| Tier | Price | Sites included | Custom domain | Custom CSS / code |
|------|-------|---------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Free | $0 | 3 | ✗ (.carrd.co subdomain) | ✗ |
| Pro Lite | $9/year | 3 | ✗ (premium URLs without branding) | ✗ |
| Pro Standard | $19/year | 10 | ✓ | ✗ |
| Pro Plus | $49/year | 25 | ✓ | ✓ (advanced settings, download sources) |

Source: [carrd.co/pricing & carrd.co/docs/pro/plans](https://carrd.co/docs/pro/plans),
confirmed across multiple reviewers.

## Free plan

- Up to 3 live sites per account
- `.carrd.co` subdomain
- Mandatory "Made with Carrd" branding footer
- Core editor features
- No custom domain
- No third-party widgets beyond what's built into Carrd Free

[source:
https://landingi.com/carrd/pricing/,
https://hamsterstack.com/pricing/carrd/]

## Pro Lite ($9/year)

- Up to 3 sites
- **Premium URLs without branding** (i.e. brand the URL you get but
  still `.carrd.co`)
- High-quality images + larger media uploads
- Access to premium and custom templates
- Video uploads + slideshows
- No custom domain

## Pro Standard ($19/year)

- Up to 10 sites
- **Custom domains** with SSL via Let's Encrypt
- Forms with email integrations
- Third-party widgets / embeds
- Site analytics
- Remove branding
- "Most popular" / mid-tier [source: reviewer articles]

## Pro Plus ($49/year)

- Up to 25 sites
- **Custom code** (advanced settings, JS / CSS injection)
- **Download unminified HTML/CSS/JS sources** (for backup, migration,
  or self-hosting)
- Custom forms with action handlers
- All Standard features
- Power-user tier

## Trial

- 7-day free trial of Pro Plus features
- **No credit card required** — key differentiation
  [source: nocode.mba]

## Transaction fees

- **No platform fees** — Carrd does not operate as a commerce platform
  by default. Creators who monetise via forms or third-party widgets
  pay those external providers' fees (Stripe, Gumroad, Patreon, etc.)
- Carrd is pure hosting + editor
- [source:
  https://checkthat.ai/brands/carrd/pricing]

## Discount / NGO pricing

- [unclear — no publicly surfaced student or NGO pricing]
- Annual billing is the only built-in discount (monthly not offered)

## Anchor pattern

- **$9/year is astonishingly low** — effectively $0.75/month.
  This is 1/10th of Linktree's Starter tier. Carrd's pricing
  philosophy is unique in the category.
- **Per-year pricing feels like ownership** — not a recurring burden.
  It's a marketing superpower.
- **Pro Plus at $49/year = $4.08/month** still undercuts every
  competitor's cheapest paid tier.

## Comparison lens

| | Carrd Pro Standard $19/yr | Linktree Starter $96/yr | Beacons Creator $100/yr |
|---|---------------------------|-------------------------|-------------------------|
| Effective monthly | $1.58 | $8 | $8.33 |
| Sites included | 10 | 1 | 1 |
| Custom domain | ✓ | ✗ | ✓ (first year) |
| Platform fees on sales | 0% (no platform fees) | 9% | 9% |
| Custom CSS | ✗ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Design flexibility | Very high | Low | Medium |

Carrd is the **cheapest way to get a custom-domain link-in-bio** by
a huge margin.
