# Carrd — landing page audit

## Hero (direct fetch)

> **"Simple, free, fully responsive one-page sites for pretty much
> anything"**
>
> "Build one-page sites for pretty much anything. Whether it's a
> personal profile, a landing page to capture emails, or something a
> bit more elaborate, Carrd has you covered."

— [source: direct fetch of carrd.co/]

## Positioning

Carrd is **not a link-in-bio tool** in the narrow sense — it's a
general-purpose **one-page site builder** that happens to be used by
many creators as a bio-link page. This is the category's most
important positioning distinction: Carrd is a tool for people who want
to BUILD, not just to DROP-AND-GO.

Positioning vocabulary:
- "Simple" — minimal editor, mostly blocks
- "Responsive" — works across all device sizes
- "Free" — core tier includes 3 live sites

## Feature sections (from direct fetch)

1. **Simple** — "Start with templates or blank page"
2. **Responsive** — "Works across all device sizes"
3. **Free** — "Up to three sites with core features included"
4. **Pro Features** (paid tier at $19/year):
   - Custom domains with SSL
   - Unlimited sites [note: actually tier-dependent — see pricing.md]
   - Forms with email integrations
   - Third-party widgets / embeds
   - Site analytics
   - Branding removal

## CTAs (direct fetch)

- **Choose a Starting Point** — appears twice (primary CTA, leads to
  template picker)
- **Log In** (header)
- **Go Pro!** — upsell CTA promoting $19/year with "Try it free for
  7 days"

## Social proof

- **No customer count or testimonial block on the homepage** (direct
  fetch confirmed absence)
- Carrd's social proof is **the sites that have been built with
  Carrd** — it's visible in the wild across indie-hacker / creator-
  economy / product-launch corners
- "Made with Carrd" badge on free-tier sites is the reverse-viral
  marketing lever

## Trust signals

- Long track record (Carrd has been around since ~2016, older than
  most competitors in this set)
- SSL via Let's Encrypt [source: landingi.com/carrd/pricing]
- Owned by Carrd Inc., independent (no acquisition drama)

## Footer (direct fetch)

- Home / Terms / Privacy / Contact
- Copyright "© Carrd Inc. All rights reserved"
- Minimal footer — consistent with Carrd's one-page-site philosophy

## Growth hooks

- **Free tier with 3 sites** — cheap PLG surface
- **7-day free Pro trial — NO credit card required** [source: Carrd
  pricing docs] — unusual in the category (most competitors require
  CC)
- **"Made with Carrd" badge on free sites** — reverse viral
- **Template gallery** — Carrd has 264 templates [source:
  Linktree-vs-Carrd comparison articles], more than any other
  competitor in this set
- **No CTA asking for email** — Carrd's homepage doesn't leak your
  email to marketing

## Audience

Indie hackers, designers, developers, creators-as-one-page-site
owners. Anti-enterprise, anti-bloat. The "hacker news" demographic.
