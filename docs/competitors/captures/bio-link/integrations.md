# Bio.link (Hopp by Wix) — integrations catalogue

## Payments
- **Stripe** — primary processor; full Stripe settings configurable
  inside Hopp [source:
  https://support.wix.com/en/article/hopp-by-wix-adding-and-managing-actions-for-link-in-bio]
- **Wix Payments** — for creators who use Wix websites alongside
  Hopp [source: Wix blog]

## E-commerce
- **Wix Stores** — native integration; product catalogue surfaces
  directly on Hopp pages via widgets
- **Shopify** — [unclear — no first-party Shopify app surfaced for
  Hopp]

## Email / contact management
- **Built-in contact management** — import/export contacts on Pro
  (basic CRM)
- **Email capture widget** — forms collecting emails to Hopp's contact
  database
- External ESPs (Mailchimp / Klaviyo / Kit) — [unclear — via Zapier?]

## Short links / URL shortener
- **Branded short-link shortener** — bundled with Pro; uses the
  creator's .bio or .link domain for custom shortlinks
- Analytics on shortlinks
- Uniqueness: no competitor in this research set bundles a full URL

  video ads that play before a link-click lands on destination
  [source: name.com/hopp direct fetch, "Pre-rolls for monetization"]

- **Customizable search engine** — Hopp's in-page search over creator's
