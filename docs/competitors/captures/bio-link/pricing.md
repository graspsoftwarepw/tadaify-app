# Bio.link (Hopp by Wix) — pricing matrix

## Tier matrix (April 2026)

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | Forever-free |
| Pro | ~$8.50–$9.99 | [unclear — annual likely cheaper] | Free .bio/.link domain included |

Sources: [Wix blog — what is Hopp](https://www.wix.com/blog/what-is-hopp),
[name.com/hopp direct fetch](https://www.name.com/hopp).

Pricing is simpler than most competitors (2 tiers only). This is
consistent with Hopp's "built for business" positioning — the friction
is feature depth not fee modelling.

## Free plan — what's included

From direct fetch of name.com/hopp:
- Customizable search engine (search over your own links)
- Intuitive editor with professional templates
- Powerful widgets (products, forms, email capture)
- Basic link-in-bio page

Implicit: Hopp branding / ads on free page (removable on Pro).

## Pro plan (~$8.50–$9.99/mo) — what's added

- **Free .bio or .link domain** included (major differentiator —
  this is a genuine "custom domain" offer, with Hopp registering the
  domain for the creator)
- Custom domain name connection (if you already own a domain)
- Short links with customisation + analytics (branded URL shortener)
- **Pre-rolls** for monetisation (Hopp-native ad format)
- Landing page builder
- Premium templates with advanced customisation
- Contact management (import/export of the email list)
- Remove Hopp branding / ads

## Transaction fees

- Hopp's monetisation model: **no documented platform fee on
  payments**
- Stripe processor fees passthrough (typical 2.9% + $0.30 US)
- Pre-roll ad revenue share [unclear — likely ad-network standard
  split]
- Source: [Wix help — Hopp Stripe settings](https://support.wix.com/en/article/hopp-by-wix-adding-and-managing-actions-for-link-in-bio)

## Discount / NGO / student pricing

- [unclear — not publicly surfaced]
- Wix ecosystem crossover might bundle Hopp with Wix subscriptions
  [unclear]

## Anchor pattern

- **Only 2 tiers** — simplest pricing in the category, with the widest
  gap between free and paid capability
- **"Free domain with Pro"** is the headline acquisition hook
- **"Pre-rolls" monetisation** is unique language in the category —
  Hopp positions itself as a **monetisable surface** without a fee
  ladder

## Comparison lens

| | Hopp Pro $8.50-$9.99 | Linktree Starter $8 | Beacons Creator $10 |
|---|---------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Free custom domain | ✓ (included .bio/.link) | ✗ | ✓ (for first year) |
| Branding removal | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| URL shortener | ✓ branded | ✗ | ✗ |

Hopp's Pro tier offers the **deepest feature set per dollar** in the
$8-10 price band.
