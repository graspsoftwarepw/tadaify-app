# Bio.link (Hopp by Wix) — templates & themes

## Theming surface

- "Professional templates" are highlighted on name.com/hopp as a free-
  tier feature
- "Premium templates with advanced customization" gated to Pro tier
- "Intuitive editor" with page-layout, design, navigation, settings
  all customisable [source: Wix blog — what is Hopp]
- Template gallery URL [unclear — not surfaced at a marketing slug like
  `/templates`]

## Customisation depth

From Wix blog + support articles:
- Shape every part of the link-in-bio (layout + design + nav +
  settings)
- Professional templates as starting points
- "Stunning templates that transform your link in bio into a seamless
  extension of your brand" [source: Wix blog]
- Hopp's "search engine" is visually part of the theme — each template
  decides how search is exposed

## Template gallery (third-party)

Because Hopp's in-product gallery doesn't leak to SEO, third-party
template builders address the "bio.link-style template" demand:

- [**Canva**'s free bio-link website templates](https://www.canva.com/website-builder/templates/biolink/)
  — Canva sells Canva-hosted bio-link pages as a Hopp competitor
- [**Jotform**'s Social Bio Link App Templates](https://www.jotform.com/app-templates/category/social-bio-link-app-templates)
- [**Envato / ThemeForest**'s Bio Link WordPress themes](https://elements.envato.com/wordpress/bio+link)
- [GitHub: **QuickBioLinks** template](https://github.com/mackenly/quickbiolinks)
  — self-hosted Instagram bio link template
- [GitHub topic **bio-links**](https://github.com/topics/bio-links)
- Self-hosted PHP scripts like [66biolinks.com](https://66biolinks.com/)

Demand exists for bio-link templates but Hopp doesn't capture the
SEO for it.
