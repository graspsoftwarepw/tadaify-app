# Milkshake — UI Audit Notes

## Desk Research Summary

Milkshake is **the mobile-only card-based outlier** in this research
set. It's an iOS/Android app with NO web editor — creators build,
customise, preview, and publish their link-in-bio entirely from their
phone. Hero: "Build a free website on your phone with Milkshake."
**5M+ downloads, 4.9-star App Store rating, 70K+ reviews** — the
best consumer-sentiment metrics in the category. Capterra 4.6/5 (290+
reviews), G2 4.3/5 (210+ reviews), press features in Webby Awards,
Vogue Codes, Lifehacker, InVision.

**Pricing**: Free (unlimited cards + links + 30-day analytics +
Milkshake branding), Lite $2.99/mo (remove branding — cheapest
branding-removal tier in category), Pro $6.99/mo or $59.99/yr
(contact forms, mailing list, SEO, Meta Pixel, 365-day analytics,
marketing campaigns), Pro+ $9.99/mo or $99.99/yr (custom domain +
GA4).

**Signature UX**: **card-based swipeable design**. Pages are stacks
of cards (about, links, recommendations, social, portfolio, media
kit, shop). Each card has its own layout and purpose. **"Shake it
up"** is a design randomiser — refresh for a new colour + font +
layout combo. Creators "shake" until they find a combo they like.

**Integrations**: Mailchimp-style mailing-list capture (Pro), Meta
Pixel (Pro), GA4 (Pro+), IG direct integration, YouTube / Spotify /
SoundCloud embeds. **No native commerce** — shop card DISPLAYS
products but does not process payments. Creators link out to Shopify /
Etsy / Gumroad / Stripe. Up to **5 websites per account** (account
switching).

**API**: none public. Mobile-app-only architecture means the creator
never even sees a URL-based admin. This is a principled choice, not
an oversight.

**Reviews**: Praised for ease-of-use, mobile-first speed, beautiful
defaults, generous free tier. Complained-about for: no web version
(dealbreaker for desktop-first creators), shallow customisation (only
colors + fonts + content), no native commerce, Linktree-wins-on-
monetisation gap.

**Strategic read for tadaify**:

1. **Card-based swipeable design is COPYABLE and proven.** Milkshake
   has spent 5 years proving creators love this pattern. Tadaify
   shipping a card-based template option (alongside classic
   link-list) captures this UX preference without the mobile-only
   constraint.

2. **Mobile-first editor is a genuine product-design choice** with
   trade-offs. Most competitors are desktop-first with a mobile
   editor-shim. A tadaify that builds a proper mobile editor
   (native app or PWA) in ADDITION to web can serve the
   Milkshake-demographic without forcing them to choose.

3. **"Shake it up" randomiser is a delightful UX surface.** Tadaify
   could ship something similar: a "surprise me" button that
   generates new theme combinations. Low engineering effort, high
   delight.

4. **Shop card that doesn't process payments is a loud user
   complaint.** Tadaify's native commerce approach (Stan-style)
   directly addresses the gap.

5. **App Store 4.9 rating is a moat even Linktree can't beat.**
   Milkshake will keep its demographic. Tadaify's opportunity is
   adjacent demographics (desktop-creators, commerce-heavy creators,
   agency-managed creators).

6. **"Beautiful by default" is the most replicable feature in
   Milkshake's toolkit.** Tadaify shipping a catalogue of
   designer-curated card templates at launch (not requiring creators
   to pick colours) matches the "no design skills required"
   promise.
