# Milkshake — landing page audit

## Hero (direct fetch of milkshake.app)

> **"Build a free website on your phone with Milkshake"**
>
> "Say more, sell more, share more. Connect your followers to all you
> offer with a beautiful website you build on your phone in minutes."

— [source: direct fetch of milkshake.app/]

## Positioning

Milkshake is **mobile-only** — it's an iOS / Android app, not a web
builder. This is THE most distinctive positioning in the research
set. You build your link-in-bio page **entirely on your phone**, in
the app, no desktop surface required.

Secondary positioning: **card-based design**, mirroring an Instagram-
Story-like swipeable experience.

## Feature sections (direct fetch)

- **Card-Based Design** — swipeable website pages, each card
  customised with text, images, videos, links
- **Mobile-First** — built entirely on phone, no coding / design
  skills required
- **Customization** — "Shake it up" feature for design options
  (colors, fonts, logos)
- **Analytics** — daily + monthly breakdowns of link clicks + card
  views
- **Multi-Platform** — IG, TikTok, other social

## Primary CTAs

- App Store download button
- Google Play download button
- No "sign up on web" CTA — forces mobile-first onboarding

## Social proof (direct fetch)

- **5M+ downloads**
- **4.9-star rating on Apple App Store**
- **70K+ reviews**
- Featured in: **Webby Awards, Vogue Codes, Lifehacker, InVision**

This is the **best-rated mobile experience in the category** —
category-high 4.9 app store rating.

## Testimonials (direct fetch)

- "This app is not only easy to use, it completely upgrades your IG
  bio"
- "You can spin up a page for your personal business in 5 minutes"
- Multiple comparisons to LinkTree, Wix, Squarespace

## Pricing anchors (direct fetch)

| Plan | Cost | Key features |
|------|------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic creation, hosting, 30 days analytics |
| Lite | $2.99/mo | Remove branding |
| Pro | $5-6.99/mo | Analytics, contact forms, SEO tools, Meta pixel |
| Pro+ | $8.33-10/mo | Custom domain, Google Analytics |

## Target segments (direct fetch)

- Influencers
- Small businesses
- Affiliates
- Artists
- Podcasters
- Creators

## Growth hooks

- **Free tier, no credit card required**
- **Freemium** encouraging upgrades
- **Social sharing** mechanics built-in
- **Account switching** — up to 5 websites per account
- **Direct Instagram integration**
- **Mobile-first** = impulse install moments on phone mid-content-
  creation

## Trust signals

- App Store 4.9 rating
- 5M+ downloads
- Award-featured (Webby)
- Press-featured (Vogue Codes, Lifehacker, InVision)
