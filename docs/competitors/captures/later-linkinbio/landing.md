# Later Link in Bio (Linkin.bio) — landing page audit

## Important framing

Later's link-in-bio product is a **feature inside Later's broader
social-media scheduler**, not a standalone link-in-bio service. Later
is primarily an Instagram/TikTok/Pinterest scheduler ($25-$200+/mo);
Linkin.bio is marketed as a bundled feature of Later subscriptions.
Free tier of Linkin.bio exists without a paid Later sub, but depth
increases materially on Later's paid tiers.

## Hero (direct fetch of later.com/link-in-bio)

> **"One link in bio, Endless Possibilities"**
>
> "Turn followers into customers with Later's link in bio tool. Use
> link in bio from Later to create a fully customizable link in bio
> page for your Instagram, TikTok, and Snap profiles."

— [source: direct fetch of later.com/link-in-bio/]

## Feature sections (direct fetch)

1. **Custom Design** — modify buttons, backgrounds, text colors,
   themes
2. **Featured Banner** — highlight top content with a banner
   directing traffic to important links
3. **Mailchimp Integration** — build email list from the link-in-bio
   page
4. **Custom SEO** — title tags + meta descriptions per page

## Social proof (direct fetch)

- **Customer logos**: Baggu, YouTube, L'Oréal, HGTV — **enterprise /
  media brands**, not the creator-economy positioning of most
  competitors
- **Testimonial**: Brock Cardiner, Editorial Director, HighSnobiety
  — "Later's link in bio landing page is a copy of the account's
  grid. This is a seamless experience..."

These signals are **more enterprise / brand-oriented** than Linktree
or Beacons. Later's paying users are brands with social-team
structures, not solo creators.

## CTAs

- Primary: **Get your free link in bio**
- Section-level: **Explore plans**
- Footer: **Claim free link in bio**

## Core capabilities

- Track sales via custom affiliate links
- **Up to 5 links per Instagram post** (Growth/Advanced plans)
- Support for Instagram, TikTok, and Snap profiles
- **Pinterest / Linkin.bio not emphasised** but Later's scheduler
  covers Pinterest

## Trust signals

- Enterprise customers (L'Oréal, HGTV, YouTube) as logos
- Deep integration with Later's social-scheduling product
- 14-day free trial on Later's paid plans (Linkin.bio is free
  standalone)

## Footer

Standard Later footer with Company / Product / Resources / Support
sections. Linkin.bio sits under "Product".

## Growth hooks

- **Free Linkin.bio tier** — forms the acquisition funnel for the
  paid Later scheduler
- **"Up to 5 links per Instagram post"** — genuinely unique feature
  (per-post link routing, not per-profile)
- **Shopify product-tagging** — creators tag Shopify products in IG
  posts and Linkin.bio auto-renders the tagged products inline
  [source: reviewer syntheses]
- **Grid-match design** — Linkin.bio page mirrors the IG grid visually,
  so the page IS the IG feed with clickable hotspots
- **14-day Later trial** — signups to Later funnel funnels into full
  paid scheduler subscription

## Segment landings

Later segments by social platform (IG / TikTok / Pinterest / LinkedIn /
Facebook scheduler pages) and by role (creator / SMB / agency /
enterprise). Linkin.bio appears on the IG + TikTok landing pages as a
feature block.
