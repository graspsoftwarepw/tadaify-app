# Taplink — landing page audit

## Hero (direct fetch of taplink.cc)

> **"Drive more leads and sales on Instagram"**
>
> "Taplink is a convenient way to manage your links and drive your
> sales directly on Instagram"

— [source: direct fetch of taplink.cc/]

## Positioning

Taplink's positioning is **Instagram-native, lead-gen-first**. Unlike
Linktree (broadly creator), Stan (mobile store), Carrd (one-page
site), Taplink explicitly brands itself as "the Instagram bio tool
for sales" — it reads more as a mini CRM / micro-landing-page tool
than a pure link list.

## Domains

Confusingly Taplink runs two top-level domains:
- `taplink.cc` — primary product marketing
- `taplink.at` — secondary marketing + blog + signup CTA lands here
  (see hero CTA "Get started for free" → `taplink.at/en`)

Likely historical/regional reasons; either redirects to the same
account system. [unclear — confirm].

## Feature sections (direct fetch)

1. **Multiple Links** — classic link-in-bio
2. **Online Messaging** — one-click WhatsApp / Viber / Messenger launch
3. **Social Networks** — smart links that open native apps
4. **Micro Landing Page** — "Create a page that will sell" with
   text, media, FAQs, countdown timers
5. **Application Forms** — "Capture leads and accept payments online"
   with CRM integration
6. **Online Store** — "Everything you need to start selling on
   Instagram" code-free

This surface is genuinely more feature-rich than Linktree's — with
CRM-adjacent capabilities (forms, lead capture, built-in customer
tracking).

## Growth hooks

- **Free plan** — forever-free, core features
- **Time-limited offer countdown** — a feature that positions Taplink
  as a conversion tool, reverse-engineering the growth hook itself
- **Lead capture → CRM** — creators who earn leads stay on Taplink
  to manage them
- **60+ payment processors** — headline supports unusual local / niche
  payment methods
- **100+ templates + 400+ themes** — one of the largest design
  galleries in the category [source:
  https://taplink.at/en/blog/what-is-taplink.html]
- **AI page generator** — generate a page from business details +
  colour choice [source: reviewer syntheses]

## Segment landings

Taplink's targeting is **geographic and vertical**:
- Heavy in Russian / CIS / Eastern European market (origin)
- Multi-language support prominent
- Small-business / e-commerce vertical (Instagram sellers, small
  shops, service providers)
