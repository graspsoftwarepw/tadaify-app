# Taplink — integrations catalogue

## Payment processors (headline: 60+)

Taplink advertises **60+ payment processors** — the widest set in
this research. Confirmed / referenced:
- Stripe
- PayPal
- Regional CIS processors (Yandex.Kassa, WebMoney, Tinkoff,
  Sberbank — historical) [unclear — exact current set]
- Local European processors (SEPA, bank transfers in multiple
  currencies) [unclear]
- Apple Pay / Google Pay via Stripe [unclear]

Source: [taplink.at/en/blog/what-is-taplink](https://taplink.at/en/blog/what-is-taplink.html)

The 60+ processor surface is Taplink's biggest integration moat.
Creators in emerging markets or with local customer bases can accept
payment methods that Linktree / Beacons / Stan simply don't support.

## Email / ESP

- Mailchimp [unclear — native or via Zapier]

  Beacons); lead-nurturing happens in external ESP

## CRM

- **Built-in Taplink CRM** — forms-captured leads land in a
  Taplink-hosted customer table with status flags
- Export to CSV [unclear]
- Zapier bridge to external CRMs (HubSpot, Pipedrive) [unclear]

## Commerce

- Digital products native (0% commission)
- Physical products [unclear — verify]
- No native Shopify storefront embed [unclear — Taplink's model is
  "your shop lives in Taplink", not "embed Shopify"]
- 60+ payment processors make Taplink a real self-contained commerce
  surface

## Total count advertised

**20+ integrations** on the homepage feature-section language. The
60+ payment processors likely counted separately.

## Gaps vs Beacons / Stan

- No native Instagram DM automation (equivalent of Beacons' Smart
  Reply or Stan's AutoDM)
- No media-kit-for-brand-deals surface
- No affiliate catalogue
- No native email sending
