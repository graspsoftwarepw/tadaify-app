# Taplink — templates & themes

## Gallery

- **100+ page templates** grouped by category
- **400+ themes** (colour + typography combinations)
- AI page generator as template alternative — generate a custom page
  by entering business details + colour scheme [source:
  https://taplink.at/en/blog/what-is-taplink.html]

Total template surface: among the largest in the category. Only
Carrd (264) and Stan have comparable depth.

  social links, messenger links, forms, digital products, countdown
  timers, FAQ accordions
- 400+ themes provide colour + typography combinations without
  requiring creator taste

## AI page generator

Unique in category: creators enter business details + colour scheme,
AI generates a full starter page. Comparable to Beacons' "Beam" but
Taplink's is specifically page-generation-first (vs Beam which is
general AI assistance).
