# Taplink — pricing matrix

## Tier matrix (April 2026)

Taplink has 3 pricing editions spanning $0 to $6/month equivalent,
with concrete tier labels varying across sources. Cross-referencing
GetApp / Capterra / G2 listings:

| Tier | Monthly | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Free | $0 | Core bio-link page with Taplink branding |
| Basic | ~$2-3/mo | Branding removed + base templates |
| Premium / Business | ~$6-8/mo | All 100+ templates, 400+ themes, forms+CRM, advanced analytics, all integrations |

Sources:
- [G2 — Taplink pricing](https://www.g2.com/products/taplink/pricing)
- [Capterra — Taplink pricing](https://www.capterra.com/p/235736/Taplink/pricing/)
- [SaaSworthy — Taplink April 2026](https://www.saasworthy.com/product/taplink)

Exact price points and tier names vary by SaaS-aggregator source.
Live audit needed for canonical pricing-page enumeration.

## Free plan

- Multiple links
- Basic templates
- Taplink branding on page
- Some integrations available
- No CRM / advanced analytics
- No commerce / no custom domain

Source: [softwaresuggest Taplink](https://www.softwaresuggest.com/taplink)

## Paid tiers (feature inventory across tiers)

From direct taplink.cc fetch + reviewer syntheses:

- **100+ page templates** across categories
- **400+ themes** with different design styles
- **AI page generator** (create a page from business details + colour
  scheme)
- **Custom domain** connection
- **Built-in CRM** — track customer status, manage leads captured
  from forms
- **Analytics** — views, link clicks, conversions
- **Digital product sales** with **0% commission** (Taplink takes
  nothing from sales)
- **60+ payment processors** — unusually wide support (includes
  regional/local processors beyond Stripe/PayPal)
- **20+ integrations** — marketing, ad tracking, sales, analytics

Source: [taplink.at/en/blog/what-is-taplink.html]

## Transaction fees

- **0% Taplink platform commission** on digital product sales
- Payment processor fees passthrough (varies by processor)
- This matches Stan Store and beats Linktree (9-12%) / Beacons (9%
  on free/creator tiers)

## Anchor pattern

- **Cheapest paid tiers in the category**: $2-3 entry, $6-8 top tier
