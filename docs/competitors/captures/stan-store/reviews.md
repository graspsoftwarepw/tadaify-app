# Stan Store — third-party reviews synthesis

## Rating snapshot (April 2026)

- **Trustpilot: 4.8/5 from 1906 reviews, ~91% 5-star** [source:
  https://www.trustpilot.com/review/stan.store]
- G2: [unclear — no strong G2 presence surfaced]
- Product Hunt: active listing
- Creator-video / TikTok review volume is **very high** —
  Stan has become a creator-economy-native recommendation
- 4.7/5 from 201 reviews on a different review aggregator (likely
  heyorai / similar) [source: search synthesis]

Stan is the **highest-rated competitor in this research set**. This is
a meaningful moat.

## Top 5 praised features

1. **Speed to launch** — "multiple reviewers noting that they had
   their store live and accepting payments within 15 to 30 minutes of
   signing up" [source: crevio.co/blog/is-stan-store-legit]
2. **Zero platform fees** — "creators appreciate keeping 100% of
   their revenue minus standard payment processing costs" [source:
   crevio]
3. **Exceptional customer service** — "exceptional customer service
   is a recurring theme in reviews" [source: crevio] (contrast with
   Beacons' 1.9/5 support signal)
4. **Mobile-first design** — stores render perfectly on phones, where
   creators' audiences actually click from bio links
5. **Bookings + products + courses all in one flow** — unified
   experience avoids Stripe/Calendly/Teachable fragmentation

## Top 5 complaints

1. **No free plan, $29/mo from day 15** — "no free plan—you pay
   $29/month from day 15, which for creators pre-revenue is a real
   barrier" [source: crevio.co/blog/is-stan-store-legit]
2. **Cookie-cutter design** — "Design is template-y—you can change
   colors, fonts, and a header image, but you cannot build anything
   that looks meaningfully different from every other Stan Store"
   [source: crevio]
3. **$99 paywall for email + funnels** — "the $99/month paywall for
   email marketing and tracking tools may frustrate creators as their
   businesses grow" [source: crevio]
4. **Thin course product** — "Course features are thin with no
   quizzes, no certificates, no progress tracking" [source: crevio]
5. **Basic analytics on entry tier** — advanced requires $99 upgrade

## Churn reasons

- Hit sales ceiling and want custom design → migrate to Shopify
- Need real course features (quizzes, certificates, drip) →
  Teachable / Thinkific / Kajabi
- Want free plan to experiment → go to Beacons or Linktree

## Pricing complaints specifically

- **$29 entry with NO free plan is the biggest blocker** [source:
  crevio]
- **$99 Pro jump is too steep** — creators feel trapped between
  "can't afford Pro" and "stuck with basic Creator" [source: crevio]

## Feature requests repeated

- More advanced course features (quizzes, certificates)
- Deeper storefront customisation / CSS access
- Free plan [frequent] or longer trial
- Email marketing at the Creator tier (not Pro-only)
- More template variety
- Better analytics on Creator tier
