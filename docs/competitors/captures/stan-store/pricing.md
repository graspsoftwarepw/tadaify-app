## Creator plan ($29/mo)

Included:
- Mobile-optimised store
- Unlimited digital products
- Unlimited courses (basic course features)
- Calendar bookings (Google Cal sync, Zoom)
- Subscriptions (recurring revenue product type)
- Community surface
- AutoDM (Instagram DM automation post-purchase)
- Google Analytics + Facebook Pixel + TikTok Pixel
- Stripe + PayPal payouts

Source: [stan.store/blog/stan-store-pricing](https://stan.store/blog/stan-store-pricing/),
[talkspresso Stan 2026](https://talkspresso.com/blog/stan-store-pricing-and-features-2026).

## Creator Pro ($99/mo)

Adds on top of Creator:
- Email Marketing (native email send surface)
- Funnels (multi-step sales paths)
- Upsells + Order Bumps
- Discount codes
- Payment plans (split pay)
- Affiliate tools (creator-recruited affiliates)
- Ad-pixel tracking extensions
- Advanced analytics

Source: same.

## Transaction fee model

- **0% Stan platform fee** on all sales — BOTH tiers
- Payment-processor fees still apply (Stripe / PayPal typical
  2.9% + $0.30 US; varies 1.5%-3.5% by country)
- Source: [crevio — is Stan legit 2026](https://crevio.co/blog/is-stan-store-legit)

## Free plan / trial

- **No free-forever plan**
- 14-day free trial, credit card required [unclear — confirm CC
  requirement]
- After trial → must convert to Creator ($29) or Creator Pro ($99)

- [unclear — not publicly surfaced; annual discount referenced but

## Anchor pattern

- **Two-tier pricing** is the simplest in the category (most
  competitors run 3-4 tiers)
- Creator Pro $99 is a significant step-up (3.4× Creator) — Pro is
  positioned as "scaling creators" with email + funnels being the
  primary upgrade hook
- No tier differentiation on transaction fees (flat 0% both plans)
  removes fee ladder as a pricing lever — Stan's sell is purely feature
  depth

## Comparison lens

| | Stan Creator $29 | Linktree Pro $15 | Beacons Creator Plus $30 |

| Seller fee | 0% | 9% | 0% |
| Digital products | unlimited | unlimited | unlimited |
| Email native | ✗ (need $99 tier) | ✗ | unlimited |
| Custom domain | ✓ [unclear which tier] | ✗ | ✓ |
| Calendar bookings | ✓ native | ✗ | ✓ native |
| Course hosting | ✓ basic | ✗ | ✓ (Plus+ with video) |

Stan's value vs Beacons Creator Plus at similar price: **Stan leads
on simplicity + pure-commerce focus**, **Beacons leads on bundled
marketing-stack depth**.

## Hidden-cost observations

- **No free plan = no viral loop / PLG** — Stan treats acquisition
  as marketing expense rather than product expense
- **$99 paywall for email marketing** is the most-cited complaint
  [source: crevio.co/blog/is-stan-store-legit]
- Creator tier analytics are "basic" per reviewer language —
  advanced analytics gated to Pro [source: same]
