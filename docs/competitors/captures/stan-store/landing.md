# Stan Store — landing page audit

## Scrape constraints

Direct fetch of `stan.store/` returned client-side-rendered shell with no
usable content; `/pricing` redirected to a "store under construction"
page, indicating stan.store is both the marketing domain AND the
template for creator stores, and the root path is reserved for user
stores. Content below synthesised from Stan's blog at `stan.store/blog/*`
(reachable), help centre at `help.stan.store`, and third-party
reviewers.

## Positioning

Stan is **not a link-in-bio tool in the traditional sense** — it
positions as "Your Creator Store", a mobile-first all-in-one sales
page. Core framing: a single mobile-shaped store where a creator's
audience pays for digital products, courses, calls, subscriptions,
community access, all without leaving the store view. Source:
[stan.store/blog/stan-store-pricing](https://stan.store/blog/stan-store-pricing/).

The link-in-bio aspect is implicit: creators put `stan.store/<name>`
in their TikTok / IG bio, and the whole funnel lives there. Stan's
marketing leans hard into **"monetise your audience"** rather than
"present your links".

## Social proof (landing)

- **4.8/5 Trustpilot** from 1906 reviews, ~91% 5-star [source:
  https://www.trustpilot.com/review/stan.store] — the highest-rated
  competitor in this research set by a wide margin
- **TikTok-creator-led word-of-mouth** — Stan is disproportionately
  recommended by creator-economy YouTubers and TikTokers
  [source: pervasive TikTok links in SERP for "Stan Store"]

## Value proposition blocks (inferred)

Stan's product-surface vocabulary:
- **Store** — the mobile-first commerce page (core)
- **Products** — digital downloads, courses, physical-merch
  integrations, memberships
- **Bookings** — calendar-connected 1:1 / coaching slots (Google Cal
  sync; Zoom for calls) [source:
  https://slashdot.org/software/p/Stan.store/integrations/]
- **Community** — gated community surface for paid members
- **AutoDM** — Instagram DM automation after-sale
- **Funnels / Upsells / Order Bumps** — paid-tier sales optimisation
- **Email Marketing** — paid-tier tier
- **Affiliate programme** — built-in on Creator Pro

## CTAs

- Secondary: **See pricing** → `/pricing` (redirects / CSR)

## Growth hooks

- **14-day free trial** — the ONLY free access
- **No free plan** — conscious differentiation vs Linktree/Beacons
  that accepts higher acquisition friction in exchange for higher LTV
  (every sign-up is a potential paid customer from day 15)
- **Affiliate programme** (Creator Pro $99) — creators can recruit
  their own affiliates, paying them commissions on their products
- **"Made with Stan" badge** [unclear — verify presence]

## Segment landings

Stan's segmentation is by product-surface not niche:
- Digital products creators
- Coaches / consultants (calendar-heavy)
- Course creators
- Community-first creators
- Content-creator-monetisation generalists
