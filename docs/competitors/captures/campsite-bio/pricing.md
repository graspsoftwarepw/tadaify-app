# Campsite.bio — pricing matrix

## Tier matrix (April 2026)

| Tier | Monthly | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Free | $0 | Unlimited links, customizable templates, Campsite branding |
| Pro | $7/mo | Custom domains, advanced analytics, media embeds |
| Pro+ | $24/mo | Teams with multiple profiles |

Sources: [entertainment.toolsinfo.com/tool/campsite-bio](https://entertainment.toolsinfo.com/tool/campsite-bio),
[toolradar.com/tools/campsite-bio](https://toolradar.com/tools/campsite-bio),
reviewer synthesis.

## Free plan

- **Unlimited links** — matches Lnk.Bio / Linktree on this
- Customizable templates (10+ templates available on free)
- Upload own images for backgrounds on free tier
- Adjust fonts on free tier
- Customize text spacing and social icon placement on free tier
- Basic click tracking
- **Prominent Campsite branding** (removable on Pro)
- Email capture opt-in forms [unclear — tier gating]

The free tier is **materially more capable than Linktree's free
tier** — this is Campsite's explicit positioning. Source:
[limey.io/blog/campsite-vs-linktree]

## Pro ($7/mo)

- Custom domains (DNS CNAME)
- Advanced analytics
- Media embeds (video, audio, interactive)
- Premium integrations
- Opt-in forms (full feature)
- Tracking pixels (GA4, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel)
- Remove Campsite branding
- UTM parameters per link

## Pro+ ($24/mo)

- **Organizations feature** — multiple profiles under one account
- Team collaboration tools
- Agency-friendly bulk editing
- Multiple admins per profile
- Priority support
- [unclear — specific seat limits]

## Annual / discounts

- [unclear — annual billing terms not surfaced]
- No lifetime deals

## Transaction fees

- **No platform fees on sales** — Campsite routes commerce through
  embedded link blocks (Gumroad / Stripe / external)

## Anchor pattern

- **$7 Pro is one of the cheapest "custom domain + analytics" tiers
  in the category** (matching Hopp Pro's $8.50)
- Pro+ at $24 targets agencies / multi-profile users — significantly
  cheaper than Linktree Enterprise sales-led pricing
- Free tier's actual usability is a marketing wedge — "more in free
  than Linktree's Starter"

## Comparison lens
