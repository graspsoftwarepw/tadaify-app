# Campsite.bio — landing page audit

## Hero (direct fetch)

> **"Create your space on the web"**
>
> "Convert more followers into customers with a link in bio tool
> created just for you."

— [source: direct fetch of campsite.bio/]

## Subhero

> **"A Blazing Fast Bio Link Made Your Way"**
>
> Emphasizes ease of creation, customization, and sharing with
> mobile optimization and quick setup.

## Feature blocks (direct fetch)

Five capability areas:
1. **Organize your bio** — links, titles, dividers, social accounts,
   groups
2. **Personalize** — profile customization
3. **Engage** — opt-in forms, carousels, feeds
4. **Auto-Pilot** — scheduling, automation
5. **Convert & Monetize** — analytics, insights

Advanced features: UTM parameters, custom domains, bulk editing,
collaborative tools.

## Social proof (direct fetch)

> **"Join over 250k creators, agencies, and brands already on
> Campsite.bio"**

**Featured clients** (direct fetch):
- **OrangeTheory Fitness**
- **Dell**
- **Participant** (Participant Media, the film studio)
- **Starlight Social**
- **Georgetown University**

This is a **stronger enterprise/agency roster than Linktree or
Beacons publicly advertise**. Campsite leans into B2B positioning
more than most competitors in the set.

## CTAs

- Primary: **Get Started for Free** (appears twice)
- Secondary: **Create Your Bio Link**, **Learn more** (analytics /
  organizations)
- Navigation: **Compare our Plans** (pricing page)

## Pricing anchors

- References to "Campsite.bio Pro" and "Pro+" tiers
- Specific pricing not displayed on homepage — invites click to
  pricing page
- Pro at $7/mo, Pro+ at $24/mo [source: reviewer syntheses;
  verified below]

## Footer (direct fetch)

- Links to support, blog, use cases (6 categories), social media,
  legal pages
- "Made with heart in Milwaukee 🇺🇸" — US indie positioning

## Trust signals

- 250k creators/agencies/brands number is solid headline
- Enterprise logos (Dell, Georgetown, OrangeTheory) signal
  B2B-grade reliability
- Milwaukee-based → independent ownership, not VC-rush

## Growth hooks

- **Referral programme** (mentioned in footer)
- **Organizations feature** for team management (agency angle)
- **Advanced analytics** as upgrade anchor
- **More generous free tier than Linktree** [consistent theme in
  reviewer comparisons]

## Segment landings

Footer lists **6 use-case categories** [unclear — enumerate: likely
creators, brands, agencies, educators, event organisers, non-profits].
Campsite explicitly markets to multiple personas rather than
creators-only.
