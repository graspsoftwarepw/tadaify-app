# Campsite.bio — UI Audit Notes

## Desk Research Summary

Campsite.bio is a **Milwaukee-based indie link-in-bio tool** that
punches above its weight with **enterprise / agency positioning**.
Hero: "Create your space on the web" + "Convert more followers into
customers." Claimed **250k creators/agencies/brands**, with
homepage logos from **Dell, Georgetown University, OrangeTheory
Fitness, Participant, Starlight Social** — a materially stronger
B2B roster than Linktree or Beacons publicly show.

**Pricing**: Free (unlimited links, 10+ templates, Campsite
branding), **Pro $7/mo** (custom domain, advanced analytics, media
embeds, pixels), **Pro+ $24/mo** (Organizations feature = multiple
profiles + team collaboration). $7 Pro is the cheapest "custom-
domain + analytics" entry in the entire research set.

**Free tier is genuinely more generous than Linktree**: upload own
background images, adjust fonts, customise text spacing and social
icon placement on FREE — all things Linktree gates to paid. This is
Campsite's single most-cited competitive advantage.

**Features**: Groups (nested link hierarchies — unusual in category),
dividers, carousels (free tier!), opt-in forms (email capture),
feeds, UTM parameters per link, scheduled links, bulk editing (Pro+),
auto-pilot automation.

**Integrations**: Mailchimp (capture), GA4, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel.
Commerce via embedded blocks (Gumroad / Stripe Link / Shopify Buy
Button). No Campsite-native checkout. No Instagram DM automation.

**Organizations (Pro+)**: agency-friendly multi-profile management.
Cheaper than Linktree Enterprise (which is sales-led custom
pricing), so Campsite captures the mid-market agency segment.

**API**: none public. Same closed-platform pattern.

**Reviews**: Trustpilot positive, customer service praised as
"consistently wonderful." Complained-about: smaller template
library (10+ vs Lnk.Bio's 368), no native commerce, smaller brand
than Linktree. Enterprise clients like Campsite's reliability and
free-tier generosity.

**Strategic read for tadaify**:

1. **Free-tier generosity is the single most-effective marketing
   message** in the category — Campsite proves it works. Tadaify
   shipping a free tier with custom backgrounds + font choice +
   layout control beats Linktree-quality free tier.

2. **"Groups" / nested link hierarchies** is a genuine UX innovation
   for creators with 20+ links. Tadaify should ship grouping /
   accordion as a first-class block.

3. **Organizations / agency tier at $24 is a concrete price anchor
   below Linktree Enterprise.** Tadaify targeting agencies should
   offer similar multi-profile + team-roles at ~$25-30 to undercut
   both.

4. **Milwaukee / indie-owned positioning resonates with
   anti-VC-creator sentiment.** Tadaify's brand could lean into
   "independent" positioning as a moral contrast with VC-backed
   Linktree / Beacons.

5. **Dell / Georgetown / OrangeTheory on the homepage proves that
   link-in-bio tools have a B2B market beyond creators.** Tadaify's
   target-market segmentation should include a "brand marketing"
   persona, not just "creators".
