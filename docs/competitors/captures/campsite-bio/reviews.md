# Campsite.bio — reviews synthesis

## Rating snapshot

- **Trustpilot**: [trustpilot.com/review/campsite.bio](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/campsite.bio) — active corpus
- **G2 / Capterra**: thinner footprint than Linktree/Beacons
- **Product Hunt**: active
- Reviewer sentiment generally positive with emphasis on free-tier
  generosity

## Top 5 praised features

1. **More generous free tier than Linktree** — "Campsite.bio stands
   out for offering more flexibility in its free tier than Linktree,
   allowing professional-looking designs without paying extra. Even in
   its free plan, Campsite.bio allows users to upload their own images
   for backgrounds, adjust fonts, and customize everything from text
   spacing to social icon placement, providing more than 10 templates"
   [source: https://limey.io/blog/campsite-vs-linktree]

2. **Enterprise-grade client roster** — "Dell, Georgetown University,
   OrangeTheory Fitness" on the homepage [source: direct fetch of
   campsite.bio/] — rare for a link-in-bio tool to feature these
   logos

3. **Wonderful customer service** — Trustpilot reviewer quote
   [source: trustpilot.com/review/campsite.bio]

4. **Performance / page load speed** — "focuses on performance and
   clean design, offering fast-loading link pages" [source:
   reviewer syntheses]

5. **Custom domains on $7 Pro tier** — cheapest custom-domain entry
   in the category

## Top 5 complaints

1. **Prominent Campsite branding on free** — "but includes prominent
   Campsite branding and limited customization" [source:
   reviewer summaries]

2. **Fewer templates than Lnk.Bio / Taplink** (10+ vs 368/100+)

3. **Fewer integrations than Beacons** (no 12k-brand affiliate
   catalogue)

4. **No native commerce** (relies on embeds)

5. **Smaller brand than Linktree** — discoverability / audience
   trust deficit

## Churn / cancellation reasons

- Creators who want native commerce migrate to Stan / Beacons
- Agencies who outgrow Organizations feature move to Linktree
  Enterprise or custom tooling
- Simplicity-seekers with one-link pages stay on Linktree for
  brand familiarity

## Pricing complaints

- Generally minimal — $7 Pro is among the cheapest in the category
- Pro+ jump to $24 is a steep multiplier (3.4×) — some users want
  a middle $15 tier

## Feature requests

- More templates
- Native commerce / payments
- Public API for agency bulk management
- AI page generator (to match Taplink / Beacons)
- Instagram DM automation
