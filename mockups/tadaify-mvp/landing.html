Flagship #1 · Privacy-first

🔒 No cookies. No tracking. Ever.

Your visitors don't see a cookie banner. ever. That means fewer distractions, better first impressions, and more conversions for you.

🔒 no cookies 👁‍🗨 no fingerprinting 🇪🇺 GDPR-clean by design 🤝 your visitors stay happy

Most platforms cookie-track your visitors — which means your audience has to dismiss a banner before seeing your page. We chose a different path.

We count unique visitors using a privacy-first daily method — no persistent ID, no browser storage. Visitors never see a consent banner on your tadaify page.

The trade-off: if the same visitor returns tomorrow, we count them again. Slightly fuzzy number — but your audience's first impression stays clean.

How it works — for the technically curious

Every day we generate a fresh random salt — it exists only in memory and is never stored. When someone visits your page, we create a temporary fingerprint from their IP address and browser, mix in the daily salt, and hash it. The result is a day-scoped count: same visitor, same day = 1 count. Same visitor, next day = fresh count.

No cookies. No localStorage. Nothing written to the browser. The daily salt means even we can't link Tuesday's visitors to Monday's. Your audience stays private, your analytics stay meaningful. See plans →
