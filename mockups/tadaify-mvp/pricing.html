Pricing FAQ

Why 0% fees on paid tiers?

Because we already charge you a subscription. Charging again on the sale is a double-dip we opt out of. Stripe’s processing fee still applies (~2.9% + 30¢) — that’s Stripe’s, not ours.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

Email us within 30 days of your first charge on any paid tier. No questions, no "reason required". Full refund, no partial prorations.

Custom domain — cross-tier math?

Free + $2/mo domain = $24/yr. Creator tier (no domain add-on) = $48/yr and includes 1 domain. So if you want just a domain, stay on Free + add-on. If you want support + analytics + AI, Creator is $2/mo more net.

Annual vs monthly — is it worth it?

Annual gives you 2 months free. If you're not sure, start monthly and switch later — no penalty, same price day 1 when you upgrade.

Enterprise / over 5 seats?

Business tier scales per seat ($8/seat beyond 5). For agencies managing 10+ client brands, reach out — we have a flat custom SOW with shared billing.

EU VAT / sales tax?

Yes, we collect EU VAT automatically for Member State buyers using Stripe Tax. Price shown excludes VAT; invoice includes both rows.
