What's coming in v2

📦

Product CRUD

Create, edit, archive products. Cover, gallery, variants (size/color), inventory tracking, SKU.
💳

Stripe Connect checkout

Native checkout with Stripe Connect. Payouts to your bank, Apple Pay / Google Pay, EU tax handling.
📋

Orders inbox

All orders in one view. Mark fulfilled, print packing slips, refund, contact buyer.
📊

Sales analytics

Revenue per product, cart abandonment, top referrer, monthly snapshots.
🚚

Shipping & digital

Physical (rates by zone) + digital (auto-deliver download link on payment).
🎁

Discount codes

Percentage + flat-amount codes, expiry, usage limits. Free Stripe automatic taxes.
