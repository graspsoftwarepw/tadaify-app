A 6-digit code from your authenticator app, in addition to your password. Adds about 5 seconds to sign-in.

  2. Tap + Add or Scan QR code.
  3. Point your phone's camera at the code on the left.
  4. Or paste the secret manually if your app doesn't scan QR.
