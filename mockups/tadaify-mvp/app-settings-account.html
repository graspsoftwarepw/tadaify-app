What happens to the old URL
For 30 days, tadaify.com/alexandra will 301-redirect to your primary page: → https://alexandra.com/ (your active custom domain — primary) After 30 days the old slug is released and someone else can claim it.
Custom domains stay bound to your account — they're not tied to your handle, so alexandra.com keeps working with no DNS change. Only the tadaify.com slug needs a redirect.
